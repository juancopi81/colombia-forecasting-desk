Given the cleaned and clustered items from today's metasource pipeline, produce a daily brief that summarizes:

- Which sources were checked and which failed
- The most important signals detected
- Topic clusters and their significance
- Possible forecastable questions raised by today's signals
- Missing evidence or sources that should be checked
- Items that appear noisy or low-confidence
